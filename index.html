    About the Playlist

    After selecting a destination, you will be presented a random playlist based on the current weather condition of your selected destination.

    The playlist's relation to the weather condition will either be based literally on the playlist's name or it's energy/vibe.

    To save the playlist, open Spotify in a new tab by clicking the logo on the Spotify Player

    Don't like the playlist? Click the button below to get a new recommendation.

    Recommended Playlist
